About

WOOSH: TRANSFORMING LOGISTICS THROUGH INNOVATION

Woosh is a cutting-edge logistics platform that redefines the concept of shipping and delivery. Operating as a micro-shipping company, Woosh leverages the underutilized capacity of private vehicles and business fleets, integrating seamlessly with map services to optimize logistics operations, providing deliveries of Less-than-Pallet-Load (LPL) shipments.

VISION

Woosh envisions a future where logistics is not only efficient but also environmentallyresponsible. We aim to be at the forefront of logistic innovations, constantly evolving to meet the dynamic needs of the market and setting new standards in the industry

MISSION

Our mission is to transform everyday commutes and idle business fleets into a powerful logistics network. This not only enhances delivery efficiency but also significantly reduces environmental impact, paving the way for a sustainable logistics model.

TARGET MARKET

Woosh is primarily aimed at businesses requiring inter-facility deliveries, including e-commerce, retail, and other sectors reliant on efficient logistics services. We also target private vehicle owners and business fleet operators as potential service providers within our network.

CURRENT STATUS

We are in the early stages of development, focusing on launching a Pilot Program, gathering invaluable feedback and implementing it into our MVP (Minimum Viable Product), and establishing partnerships with potential clients and service providers.
