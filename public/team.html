Team

Ivan Bondarenko

CO-FOUNDER | CEO

Ivan Bondarenko serves as the Chief Executive Officer and Co-Founder at Woosh Logistics Inc., where he leads the strategic vision and execution of the company’s innovative approach to sustainable micro-shipping. With a strong background in mechanical engineering and robotics, Ivan has co-founded and led several startups, including Perpetua Industries, where he developed advanced CNC milling machines. He also brings creative leadership experience from The Airbrush Company, collaborating with global brands like Adidas and Nike. Ivan holds a Master’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley, specializing in robotics and autonomous systems. His blend of technical expertise and entrepreneurial experience positions him to lead Woosh’s growth in the logistics sector.

Edgar Meyroyan

CO-FOUNDER | CLO

Dr. Edgar Meyroyan serves as the Chief Legal Officer and Co-Founder at Woosh Logistics Inc., where he oversees all legal aspects of the company’s operations. With a distinguished career spanning over seven years as a Legal Assistant Manager at Ernst & Young CJSC (EY Armenia), he brings a wealth of expertise in corporate legal matters, encompassing both national and international domains. Dr. Meyroyan holds a PhD in Law from prestigious institutions in Italy and Armenia, reflecting a profound understanding of diverse legal systems. Currently, he is further enhancing his expertise at UC Berkeley Law, specializing in business and corporate law, as well as international and comparative legal studies. The multifaceted background, coupled with extensive experience and academic excellence, positions Dr. Meyroyan as a formidable legal professional adept at navigating complex legal landscapes and driving strategic legal initiatives.

Steve Walsh

INDUSTRY ADVISOR

Steve Walsh is a Bay Area native and transportation logistics professional with over 35 years of experience in global logistics management and solutions. Steve has been recognized and received multiple awards for his leadership and innovation. Proven success in solving complex logistics challenges and providing innovative performance-based solutions in a variety of diverse vertical markets. Air charter, Cold Chain, Tradeshow, Heavyweight Air charter, Air export/Air import, Ocean, FTL, LTL Domestic air, Distribution, warehousing, and inventory management.

Rupesh Shrestha

ENGINEERING PM

Rupesh Shrestha is a Mechanical Engineer with a background in product development and design. He holds a Master of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from UC Berkeley, specializing in Product Design. Rupesh brings a wealth of technical expertise to his role as Engineering Project Manager at Woosh Logistics Inc., where he leads the development of innovative, IoT-enabled secure boxes for the company’s logistics solutions. His experience spans diverse environments, from desk work to manufacturing floors, with a focus on engineering design. Beyond his engineering career, Rupesh has held leadership positions, contributing to various developmental initiatives that blend technology and community impact. In his free time, he enjoys hiking, drawing, cooking, and immersing himself in music and the arts.
